## Creating Your Own Test Data

For best results, job descriptions should:
- List specific technical skills
- Mention required years of experience
- Include both required and nice-to-have skills
- Be at least 200-300 words
- Use clear section headers

## Expected Analysis

When you test with these JDs, you should see:
- **Match Score**: 40-80% depending on your background
- **Extracted Skills**: 10-20 technical skills
- **Missing Skills**: Skills you don't have but JD requires
- **Strengths**: Areas where you excel
- **Suggestions**: Specific improvements to increase match
